# Maps & Objectives

## Map Principles
- **Compact arenas + verticality** to emphasize movement skill.
- **Readable silhouettes** to keep combat legible at speed.
- **Risk vs. reward lanes** for different weapon ranges.

## Objectives
- Rotating control points or nodes to keep tempo high.
- Objectives that buff minion efficiency when controlled.
