# Player & Combat Design

## Player Skill Expression
- **Movement mastery:** air-strafe, slide, wall interactions, or hop-boosting.
- **Precision gunplay:** projectile travel, recoil patterns, and predictive aim.
- **Decision speed:** rapid target selection and tactical repositioning.

## Combat Goals
- Player actions should feel decisive in every engagement.
- Minions should support, setup, and amplify the playerâ€™s actions.

## Weapon & Ability Principles
- Baseline weapons should be reliable and skill-rewarding.
- Abilities should create short, high-impact windows for minion synergy.
