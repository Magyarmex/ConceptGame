# Economy & Progression

## Resource Flow
- **Steady income** plus performance bonuses.
- Resource spend decisions should be **short-term and frequent**.

## Spend Options
- Spawn minions vs. personal power upgrades.
- Short-term pressure vs. long-term scaling.

## Anti-Snowballing
- Diminishing returns on resource advantages.
- Objective bonuses provide catch-up opportunities.
